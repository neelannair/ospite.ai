## TIPS FOR SENDING

1. **Personalise before sending.** Look up the property, mention something specific — their location, a detail from their listing, a recent review. Generic emails get deleted.

2. **Lead with the problem, not the product.** "Your guests are awake at 2am" is more compelling than "I've built an AI tool."

3. **Include a demo link.** A live demo does the selling. Set up a pre-loaded version of the assistant and link directly to it.

4. **WhatsApp over email for follow-up.** In Italy, Spain, and Portugal, WhatsApp is the professional communication channel for small businesses. After the first email, follow up on WhatsApp.

5. **Offer scarcity.** "I'm doing 3 pilot setups this month" is true and creates appropriate urgency.

6. **Target independently-owned properties.** Boutique hotels, agriturismos, casas rurais, family-run B&Bs. Chains have their own teams. Independent owners are the ones who need you.
